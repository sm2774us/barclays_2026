LIQUID FINANCING — FINANCING RATE & REBATE POLICY (v4)
Effective: 2026-01-01

1. GENERAL COLLATERAL (GC) RATE
The GC rate benchmark is set daily off the prevailing overnight SOFR fixing plus a
desk-determined spread reflecting term funding conditions. Published to the desk by 7:00am ET.

2. SPECIALNESS FEE DETERMINATION
For hard-to-borrow (HTB) securities, the specialness fee is quoted as an annualized
basis-point spread over GC, determined by the desk's fee forecasting model with a
mandatory trader review for any quote where the model's predicted 80% interval spans
more than 50bps.

3. REBATE RATE (CASH COLLATERAL)
Rebate rate = GC rate - specialness fee, floored at zero for securities trading special.

4. QUARTER-END / MONTH-END ADJUSTMENT
Financing rates quoted within the two business days preceding a quarter-end are subject
to a documented balance-sheet scarcity premium, communicated via the morning desk note.

5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any client dispute regarding a quoted financing rate must be escalated within 2 business
days with reference to the specific model version and input snapshot used for the quote.
